What's Not Covered

To keep things transparent, here's what falls outside our warranty coverage. We believe in being upfront — no surprises, ever.

  • Normal wear patterns in high-traffic areas (walkways, play zones)
  • Damage from window or door reflections (magnified heat)
  • Weed growth through or around turf edges
  • Pet odors or staining (maintenance tips provided at install)
  • Damage caused by humans, pets, or wildlife (tears, burns, etc.)
  • Acts of nature — storms, flooding, lightning, earthquakes
  • Turf matting or flattening from heavy furniture left in place
  • Changes to irrigation systems made after installation
  • Damage from chemicals, paint, solvents, or fire
  • Underground utility or wire damage during third-party work
  • HOA disputes, zoning violations, or municipal ordinance issues
  • Modifications or repairs made by anyone other than LawnLogic
Not sure if your issue is covered? Don't stress — just submit a claim below or give us a call at (706) 701-8873. We'll always give you an honest answer and do our best to help, even if it's outside warranty.
