Frequently Asked Questions About Rooftop Green Space

How is rooftop turf different from ground-level?

+

Rooftop installations use lightweight drainage systems and specialized base materials to minimize structural load.

Will rooftop turf damage my building?

+

No. We engineer rooftop systems to distribute weight safely. Professional installation ensures no damage.

Can I add seating and features to my rooftop?

+

Yes! We coordinate with contractors to add furniture, fire pits, planters, and other rooftop features.

How do you handle drainage on a roof?

+

We work with existing roof drainage systems and add additional engineered drainage layers for proper water management.

Is rooftop turf maintenance different?

+

Maintenance is similar to ground-level turf—occasional rinsing and light grooming keeps it perfect.
