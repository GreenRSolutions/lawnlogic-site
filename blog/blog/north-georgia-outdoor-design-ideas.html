10 Outdoor Design Ideas for North Georgia Homes

By Dusty Broadhead | March 16, 2026

North Georgia's climate, natural beauty, and outdoor lifestyle offer amazing opportunities for creating exceptional outdoor spaces. After 20+ years designing and installing landscapes in the Kennesaw area and beyond, I've learned what works beautifully in our region. Let me share 10 design ideas that transform Georgia backyards into spaces people actually live in and love.

1. Multi-Zone Entertaining Spaces

Design your yard with different zones: a patio dining area, a lounge seating zone with shade, and a games area on artificial turf. This creates flow and lets guests move naturally. LawnLogic Turf uses artificial turf to connect these zones visually while providing practical surfaces for different activities.

2. Shade Structures for Georgia Heat

North Georgia summers are brutal. A well-designed pergola, shade sail, or arbor creates usable outdoor space during hot months. Combine with artificial turf underneath for a complete cool-weather gathering space. Many customers add a misting system for additional comfort.

3. Fire Pits with Seating Areas

A fire pit with surrounding seating creates an intimate gathering space. Pair with artificial turf that stays beautiful year-round. Evening conversations around a fire pit with maintained landscapes is quintessential North Georgia living.

4. Water Features and Ponds

A small pond, fountain, or water feature adds life and sound to your landscape. The visual and auditory elements transform outdoor spaces. Artificial turf surrounding water features creates lush settings that work beautifully.

5. Raised Garden Beds with Turf Surrounds

Growing vegetables or ornamental plants in raised beds lets you control soil and growing conditions. Surround with artificial turf for low-maintenance landscape appearance. This works beautifully for hobby gardeners.

6. Outdoor Kitchen and Grilling Stations

A complete outdoor kitchen with grill, counter space, storage, and seating creates the ultimate entertaining space. Artificial turf surrounding the area stays perfect and beautiful regardless of use.

7. Slope Solutions with Terracing

Many North Georgia properties have significant slope. Terracing with retaining walls and artificial turf converts challenging slopes into usable, beautiful spaces. Each level can serve different purposes—entertaining, planting, or recreation.

8. Dog Runs and Pet Play Areas

Dedicated pet play areas with durable artificial turf let dogs play safely and happily. Pet-friendly turf drains properly and handles dog use beautifully. Enclosed areas keep dogs secure while they exercise.

9. Lighting Design for Evening Use

Landscape lighting extends outdoor living into evening. Path lighting, accent lighting on trees, and decorative lights create ambiance and safety. Properly lit artificial turf creates stunning nighttime aesthetics.

10. Native Plant Integration

Incorporate native Georgia plants—flowering shrubs, ornamental grasses, native trees—into your landscape. These thrive in our climate with minimal care. Combine with artificial turf for a landscape that's both beautiful and functional.
