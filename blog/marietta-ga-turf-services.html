Marietta’s Soil Challenges

Marietta sits on some of the heaviest red clay in Cobb County. This clay presents specific challenges for both natural grass and turf installation. For natural grass, the clay’s poor drainage leads to standing water, root rot, and chronic thin patches. For turf installation, the clay requires deeper excavation and more robust base preparation to ensure long-term drainage performance.

Our experience with Marietta’s soil means we know exactly how to prepare the base for lasting results. We excavate deeper in heavy clay areas, use thicker aggregate layers, and design drainage solutions that account for clay’s impermeability. This local soil knowledge is one of the biggest advantages of working with a Kennesaw-based installer versus a company from outside the area.
