Not a Franchise. Not a Corporation. Your Neighbors.

LawnLogic started because too many homeowners were getting burned — big franchise crews that overpromised, cut corners, and disappeared when something went wrong. We're the opposite of that.

I'm Dusty. My wife Haylee and I built this business from the ground up — literally. We started with a truck, a dream, and 20+ years of hands-on experience in outdoor construction. We've poured concrete in the rain, laid turf in 100-degree heat, and stayed until midnight to make sure a customer's yard was perfect for their kid's birthday party the next morning. That's just how we operate.

We're raising our family right here in Kennesaw. When you call LawnLogic, you're not getting a call center — you're getting me, Haylee, or someone who's been with us for years. We know our neighbors by name. We coach their kids' teams. We go to the same churches and shop at the same stores. Your yard is our reputation, and in a small community, reputation is everything.

"We don't do this for the money. We do it because there's nothing like seeing a family step onto their new yard for the first time and watching their kids lose their minds."

— Dusty
