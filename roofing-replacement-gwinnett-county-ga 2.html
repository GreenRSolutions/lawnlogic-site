Frequently Asked Questions About Roofing Replacement Services

How long does a roof last?

+

Asphalt shingles last 15-20 years; metal roofs 40-70 years; slate and tile 50-100+ years. Material choice affects longevity.

What roofing materials do you offer?

+

We install asphalt shingles, metal roofing, slate, tile, cedar shake, and synthetic materials for various budgets and styles.

How long does roof replacement take?

+

Most roof replacements take 2-5 days depending on roof size and complexity. We work efficiently with minimal disruption.

Do you handle roof repairs too?

+

Yes! We repair leaks, damaged shingles, flashing issues, and other roof problems before recommending full replacement.

Is roof replacement covered by homeowners insurance?

+

Coverage depends on your policy and the cause of damage. We'll help document damage for insurance claims.
